Raft Birthday

Dearest Friends and Esteemed Acquaintances,

It is with great pleasure and a healthy dose of sarcasm that I, Lord Maximus, cordially invite you to partake in a most unconventional soiree. On Saturday, the 10th of June, we shall temporarily abandon the comforts of our stately homes and embark on a most peculiar adventure, a day trip on a raft, of all things, on a quaint little lake near Berlin. This grand escapade shall be filled with aquatic merriment and a delightful spirit of lake and water adventure.

The gathering shall commence between the hours of 10 and latest 11 in the morning at a location to be disclosed posthaste. Punctuality, as always, is of the utmost importance, for we mustn't waste a moment of this extraordinary day.

Now, do pay close attention, for I shall require each of you to bring your own sustenance (that's food, for those unfamiliar with the word) to be grilled upon a rather splendid barbecue. Additionally, please ensure you bring your own libations (yes, I'm referring to drinks) to quench your thirst in the most becoming manner. I will try to please you with some of the finest Italian delights, such as aperitivo and snacks as well, of course.

As we shall be frolicking in the sun and water, do remember to bring sunscreen, your swimming attire or other swimming accessories, lest you suffer the indignity of a sunburn or an impromptu dip in your finest garments. I eagerly await your attendance at this most unusual and, dare I say, amusing gathering. Until then, I remain

Yours sarcastically, Lord Maximus.
